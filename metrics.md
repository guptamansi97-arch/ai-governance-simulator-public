# Simulation Metrics Methodology

The simulator utilizes both qualitative and quantitative assessments to evaluate the health of an AI initiative.

## 1. Final Consensus (Qualitative)
- **Source**: Generated by the LLM during the **Round 2 Analysis** step.
- **Definition**: A professional assessment of the debate’s outcome, indicating whether stakeholders moved toward a shared decision or remained polarized.
- **Values**:
  - **High**: Strong agreement or clear convergence on a path forward.
  - **Moderate**: General agreement with specific dissenting concerns or conditional requirements.
  - **Low**: Significant friction, unresolved contradictions, or fundamental stakeholder disagreement.

## 2. Alignment Score (Quantitative)
- **Definition**: Measures the degree of consensus among all registered agents using a statistical variance model.
- **Calculation**: Uses population variance of numeric risk levels extracted from the structured Round 2 Analysis.
- **Scale**: 0 to 100.
  - **100**: Complete consensus.
  - **Lower**: High fragmentation/variance.
- **Formula**: `max(0, 100 - (variance * 40))`

## 2. Fragility Index
- **Definition**: Measures the sensitivity of the governance structure to external shocks.
- **Calculation**: Tracks the normalized delta in risk levels between Round 1 (baseline) and Round 3 (post-shock), with penalties for decision shifts.
- **Scale**: 0 to 100.
  - **0-30**: Resilient.
  - **60+**: Highly Fragile.

## 2. Risk Breakdown
Organizes post-shock agent risk assessments by organizational domain.

**Data Source**: Round 3 (post-shock) results, with Round 1 as a fallback if an agent is absent from Round 3.

**Domain Mapping**: Derived dynamically from `agent_registry.AGENTS`. Each agent has a `domain` field (e.g., `financial`, `compliance`, `operational`, `strategic`) that maps their evaluation to an organizational risk category.

**Output structure per domain**:
```json
{
  "financial": { "severity": "Moderate", "primary_concerns": ["..."] },
  "compliance": { "severity": "High",    "primary_concerns": ["..."] }
}
```

**UI**: Displayed in the Overview tab as both a horizontal severity bar chart ("Risk Posture") and a per-domain card grid ("Risk Breakdown by Domain") showing severity and top concerns.

---

## 3. Decision Recommendation
Determines the final executive stance using a two-stage evaluation model.

### Stage 1: Reinforced Hard Risk Gate (Mandatory Disqualifiers)
The simulator immediately issues a **Do Not Proceed** recommendation only in cases of severe governance failure:
- **Major Risk Polarization**: **Any** agent reports **"Very High"** risk AND **at least one other** agent reports **"High"** risk or above post-shock.
- **Structural Collapse**: **Two or more** agents shift their position to **"Becomes No-Go"** after the stress test.

### Stage 2: Calibrated Score-Based Decision
If the disqualifiers are not met, a weighted score is calculated:
`Score = 0.4 × AvgRisk + 0.3 × Fragility + 0.3 × AlignmentPenalty`

**Pilot Mitigation**: If the "Pilot" requirement is triggered (see Stage 3), the effective score is **reduced by 10 points** before mapping to the final decision.

**Decision Thresholds**:
| Score | Outcome | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| < 30 | **Proceed** | Strong alignment and resilience. |
| 30 – 80 | **Conditional Proceed** | Most realistic outcome; requires specific mitigations or pilot. |
| > 80 | **Do Not Proceed** | Extreme risk trajectory or structural collapse. |

### Stage 3: Rollout Strategy (Pilot Extraction)
The simulator deterministicially extracts conditions from the **Round 2 Analysis**.
- **Pilot Condition**: If **two or more agents** recommend a "Pilot" during the debate, the decision is flagged as **Conditional Proceed** (unless a Stage 1 rejection occurs), and a mandatory condition is added: *"Pilot deployment required before full rollout."*
